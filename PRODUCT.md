# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Foxbet operators and engineers watching a live Chicago Cubs game in person. They need to understand, from a phone in bright outdoor light, which dev gamestate feed observes the same baseball transition first and whether the evidence is strong enough to trust.

## Product Purpose

Compare every MLB-capable Redpanda gamestate stream during one selected Cubs game. The product aligns equivalent game transitions, shows raw coverage and freshness, and reports pairwise latency only when the sample is directionally consistent. Success means an operator can distinguish a genuinely faster feed from a tie, ambiguous evidence, or an identity/coverage failure.

## Brand Personality

Field-ready, empirical, calm. The voice is concise and operational: it favors evidence over declarations and makes uncertainty explicit.

## Anti-references

Not a generic dark observability dashboard, a sports-betting scoreboard, or a collection of oversized vanity metrics. Avoid decorative animation, low-contrast stadium aesthetics, unexplained winner badges, and color-only status communication.

## Design Principles

1. Put the live game and connection state before analysis.
2. Make uncertainty a first-class result, never a footnote.
3. Preserve raw evidence behind every aggregate.
4. Optimize the primary path for one-handed use in direct sunlight.
5. Use consistent source identity and transition language throughout.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, keyboard operation, focus visibility, and semantic structure. Never rely on red/green alone. Respect reduced motion and keep touch targets at least 44 pixels on compact screens.
